Short Summary of Life

I was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY until I was about 3 years old. When I was 3 my family and I moved to Staten Island, and have been living here since then. I attened P.S.50 for elementary school, I.S.24 for middle school, New Dorp High School for high school, and now am getting my bachelors degree in CSI. Over my life I have played baseball and basketball in summe rleagues, and played varsity lacrosse in high school. I have been working at a pizzeria on the island for about a year and a half.
